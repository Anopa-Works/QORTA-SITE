Kitchen & Operations

Orders arrive instantly in the kitchen

The moment a customer confirms their order, it appears on the kitchen screen. No walking back and forth. No lost paper tickets.

Staff see clear, readable tickets

Every order shows the table number, items ordered, and any customizations. No more squinting at handwriting or guessing what "no onyuns" means.

Orders move through simple states

New orders appear in a queue. Staff tap to mark them as "preparing" or "ready." Customers see these updates instantly on their phones.

Menu updates are controlled by the restaurant

Add items, change prices, mark things as unavailable. Updates appear immediately on the customer-facing menu. No waiting for someone to print new menus.

Order history is automatically tracked

See what sold, when it sold, and how long orders took. Useful for understanding busy periods and popular items.

What QORTA Doesn't Do

We built QORTA to solve one problem well: getting orders from customers to kitchens without mistakes. Here's what we intentionally left out:

Payment processing

Customers pay at the counter or table, just like always. QORTA handles ordering, not billing.

Reservations or table management

QORTA works for dine-in and takeout orders. If you need reservation software, you'll need a separate tool.

Inventory management

You can mark items as unavailable, but QORTA doesn't track ingredient stock levels.
