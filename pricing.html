Common Questions

Do I need to buy tablets or hardware?

No. The kitchen screen works on any device with a browser. Customers use their own phones.

What if I want to cancel?

You can cancel anytime. Monthly plans have no cancellation fee. Annual plans are non-refundable but you can use the service until the end of your paid period.

Does QORTA handle payments?

No. QORTA handles ordering only. Customers pay at the counter or table as usual.

Is there a limit on orders?

No. You can process unlimited orders. The price is the same whether you do 50 orders or 500.

Can I try it before paying?

Yes. Request a demo and we'll set up QORTA with your menu so you can test it before committing.
